# Product Backlog: Otulenie Website

**Created**: 2026-03-09  
**Source**: Product description from user input

---

## Product description

A high-fidelity, cinematic landing page for Otulenie, a mobile massage business in Wrocław. The site acts as a premium digital instrument featuring calm, grounding wellness aesthetics, specifically designed to convert visitors into booked appointments on Booksy.

---

## Business problem

Otulenie needs a strong, premium online presence to establish trust and clearly communicate its unique mobile massage service in Wrocław. The website must effectively target desk workers and busy mothers, funneling them smoothly toward direct booking outcomes while conveying a deeply relaxing, high-end experience without relying on generic web patterns.

---

## Actors

- **Prospective Client**: Browses services, consumes trust-building content, and books massage appointments via Booksy.
- **Massage Practitioner (Admin)**: Operates the business, receiving and managing bookings generated by the website.
